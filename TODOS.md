## Gap Alert Email Notifications
**What:** Email creators when a high-score opportunity (gapScore > 80) appears in their niche.
**Why:** Turns /trending from a page you visit into a service that comes to you. Massive retention potential.
**Pros:** High engagement, creators don't need to remember to check /trending, viral potential ("I got alerted to this topic before anyone else").
**Cons:** Requires notification infrastructure (email provider, preferences UI, opt-in flow, deliverability). Risk of notification fatigue if thresholds aren't tuned.
**Context:** Deferred from CEO review of Trending Command Center (2026-03-25). Build AFTER validating that creators actually use /trending regularly — building notifications before proving the page is valuable is premature.
**Effort:** L (human: ~2 weeks / CC: ~1 hour)
**Priority:** P2
**Depends on:** Trending Command Center shipped + external user validation
